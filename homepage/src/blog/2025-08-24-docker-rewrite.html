Docker Rewrite: Modernizing Our Container Infrastructure

Learn how we modernized our Docker infrastructure with improved performance, security, and developer experience. Discover the challenges we faced and the solutions we implemented.

Our journey to modernize our Docker infrastructure began when we realized that our existing setup was becoming a bottleneck for our development team. What started as a simple containerization solution had grown into a complex web of custom scripts, outdated base images, and inconsistent deployment patterns.

The Challenge

We were facing several critical issues:

  • Build times had increased from 2 minutes to over 15 minutes
  • Security vulnerabilities in outdated base images
  • Inconsistent environments between development and production
  • Complex multi-stage builds that were hard to maintain

Our Solution

We decided to take a comprehensive approach to modernizing our Docker infrastructure. This involved:

  1. Upgrading to the latest LTS base images
  2. Implementing multi-stage builds with proper layer caching
  3. Adding security scanning to our CI/CD pipeline
  4. Standardizing our Dockerfile patterns across all services

Results

The results exceeded our expectations:

  • Build times reduced by 70%
  • Image sizes decreased by 40%
  • Zero critical security vulnerabilities
  • Developer onboarding time cut in half

What's Next

This Docker rewrite is just the beginning. We're now exploring container orchestration solutions, implementing automated scaling, and looking into serverless container platforms for our next phase of infrastructure modernization.

Stay tuned for more updates on our infrastructure journey, and feel free to reach out if you're facing similar challenges in your own projects.
